About

Hey y'all, my name's Prima! I am currently an undergraduate student studying Graphic Information Technology at Arizona State. My main focus with my studies is to get my foot in the door with UI/UX design. What better way to do that, than to craft a portfolio website (like, the one you are on right now...) from scratch?

I have had a blast crafting this site and finally putting my creative skills to good use. Not only that, but my personal productivity levels have skyrocketed! This website will continue to grow and evolve, as will I, as time elapses. Thanks for following along.
