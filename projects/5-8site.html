In summer 2024, I had the opportunity to become a web design intern at Phoenix College. During this time, I collaborated with a team to craft a website aimed at teaching software development to children. Geared towards the age demographic of kids in 5th through 8th grade, the foundations of computer programming were wrapped up in an easy-to-digest package. By integrating games and interactive elements, while also keeping color theory, accessibility, and tech justice at the forefront, a high-fidelity wireframe was put together in Figma and presented to a panel of mentors and peers.
